<threat_model>
## Trust Boundaries

| Boundary | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| Privileged DBus writer → daemon tuning methods | Root-authorized calls can change live tuning behavior and persisted draft settings. |
| Auto-tune mode → managed fan hardware | The daemon temporarily overrides normal control for one managed fan during the observation window. |

## STRIDE Threat Register

| Threat ID | Category | Component | Disposition | Mitigation Plan |
|-----------|----------|-----------|-------------|-----------------|
| T-03-07 | Elevation of privilege | auto-tune and profile mutation methods | mitigate | Guard `start_auto_tune`, `accept_auto_tune`, and `set_draft_fan_control_profile` with `require_authorized()` and preserve read-open status for inspection only. |
| T-03-08 | Denial of service | auto-tune task mode | mitigate | Limit auto-tune to one managed fan per request, cap duration at 30 seconds, and abort on unreadable sensors without touching applied gains. |
| T-03-09 | Tampering | draft staging of proposals | mitigate | Stage proposals into `config.draft` only and require the existing `apply_draft` path for live promotion. |
</threat_model>
